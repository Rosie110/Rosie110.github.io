Skin Hunger

The film ‘Skin Hunger’ is an “exploration into the yearning for human touch” The counterpoint within the film is the introduction of technology as a restricting force within our modern day tech-focused lives. Within my project I wanted to take a closer look at the different types of emotions expressed through moments of touch.

I decided upon using particle like imagery to emulate nerve endings/ receptors within our body that respon to touch. I used electronic sounds ranging from ambient to corse to discomforting to match the clear narratives
of love, anger and fear.
