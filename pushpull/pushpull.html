Push and Pull

This project was undertaken at the end of second year. The brief required us to take an essay and turn it into a piece of catalogue design. The essay I chose encapsulated the silkscreen poster protest movements in the 1960’s focusing on 3 main struggles,

the Atelier Populaire in Paris, The People’s Democracy in Northern Ireland and the Black Panther movement in America. The catalogues visual language is inspired by the rough textures, bold lettering and abstract shapes/symbols of the era.
