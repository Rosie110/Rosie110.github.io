Changing Futures

The Child and Family Agency (TUSLA) set us a brief to come up with a new concept and design for a new section of their website which would be child friendly. The client and user testing groups played an active role in the design.

The visual language and tone of voice was to encompass ideas surrounding authenticity. We were required to portray the notion of “for children, made by children”. This project was completed in third year.
